ABOUT US

est. 2024

Snoopy's Snowy Cafe was opened August 27, 2024 by co-founders Snoopy and Charlie Brown. After suffering a devastating shortage in the aesthetic cafe economy, our co-founders eagerly took matters into their own hands, starting a cozy and nostalgic home for all coffee and sweet-treat enjoyers to visit.

Originally founded as a cozy corner shop, Snoopy's Snowy Cafe, although remaining in the same friendly neighbourhood location, has expanded from selling just coffee and cake to serving many customer favourites, providing Snoopy merchandise and hosting children's birthday parties.

Visit our menu now, or shop Snoopy at our in-person locations! Take a break and enjoy our signature Snowcrumble Pastry. See you then!
